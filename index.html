Unapologetically Misunderstood

Michelle Angelo’s captivating novel, Unapologetically Misunderstood, grasps the reader with a drama-filled plot.
Being a successful entrepreneur and owner of a freelance design company, Jonae has the world at the palm of her hands. After a family secret gets revealed, Jonae begins battling demons. From old childhood memories, to a relationship entanglement and beyond, she quickly begins losing control of every aspect of her life.
Unapologetically Misunderstood, is a steamy, fast-paced novel told by a cast of characters that will keep you on your toes from beginning to end. These characters are left to decide if they will surrender to their lustful cravings, make a complete break away from the life they have come to know, or boss up and be the people they have worked so hard to become.
